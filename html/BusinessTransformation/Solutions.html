Loan Management

LENDS

IA Innovations offers LENDS (Loans End to End System), an easy-to-use, cloud-based business management software for loan acquisition, servicing, and collection. This digital business solution enables organizations to offer loan products ranging from microfinancing and retail loans to more complex business loans.

We provide paperless application processing and quick credit adjudication for customer acquisition, along with smooth loan processing and credit approval workflows.
