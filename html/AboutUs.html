Our Philosophy

Our approach and methodology are based on socio-techno principles and discipline. This philosophy focuses on blending technology to an individual and an organization’s routine to ensure a higher rate of adoption of technology, thus bringing forth more positive impact.

I-Asiatic Innovations Phils., Inc., known as IA Innovations is an IT Systems Integrator and Consulting Services company founded in 2008. IA Innovations began its partnership with IBM in June 2012 specializing in Collaboration, Integration, and Analytics Solutions. Within six months, it achieved IBM the status of an Advanced Business Partner from sales and technical certifications, as well as successful client engagements.

IA Innovations is a growing business solutions and services company. We have worked with clients in various sectors, namely Banking, Financial Services, Retail, Manufacturing, Distribution, Education, and the Public Sector. Our areas of expertise are in Marketing Automation, ECommerce, Analytics/Business Intelligence, Web Development, Knowledge and Change Management, and Business Process Management.

We are experts in designing, building, and delivering solutions that work well, look good and are easy to use. We make sure our clients get maximum ROI by using the latest yet proven technologies to achieve their goals faster, better, and cheaper. At IA Innovations, we believe that information plus action equals innovation.
